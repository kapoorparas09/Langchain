**The Song of the Cricket Field**

Under the sun on a grassy stage,
Heroes gather, young and sage.
A leather ball, a willow blade,
Where dreams of glory are often made.

The bowler runs with thunder’s pace,
Determination on his face.
The batsman waits, calm and still,
A clash of courage, strength, and skill.

The crowd erupts with every cheer,
Each boundary bringing victory near.
A spinning ball, a soaring six,
A moment’s magic the game can fix.

Fielders dive like falcons in flight,
Guarding runs with all their might.
One sharp catch, the stadium roars,
As hope and heartbreak trade their scores.

From dusty streets to grand arenas,
Legends rise from simple dreams.
Cricket binds both hearts and lands,
A sport where passion proudly stands.

So let the bat sing loud and clear,
Let the ball race far and near.
For in this game, beneath blue skies,
The spirit of cricket forever flies.
